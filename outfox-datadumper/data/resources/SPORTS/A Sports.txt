A Sports is a Pakistani sports channel. It was launched on October 16, 2021 and is a part of ARY Digital Network which earlier planned in 2016. On 9 October 2021, Wasim Akram, Waqar Younis , Wahab Riaz, Misbah ul Haq announced to join as its sports analysts.
